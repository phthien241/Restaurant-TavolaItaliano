Awards
At Tavolai Italiano, we've been honored with numerous awards, including prestigious Michelin stars, a Wine Spectator Award of Excellence for our extensive wine selection, and TripAdvisor's Certificate of Excellence. We've also been recognized as the 'Best Fine Dining Restaurant' by Gourmet Traveller and received a Culinary Innovation Award for our creative approach to Italian cuisine. These accolades showcase our commitment to culinary excellence, outstanding service, and the satisfaction of our valued customers
Branches
Our restaurant chain has a strong presence in Australia, with multiple branches across key cities and regions. Each branch is strategically located to provide easy access to our customers. Whether you're in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, or other major Australian cities, you can enjoy the same high-quality dining experience and exceptional service that has made us a favorite among locals and tourists alike. Our diverse menu and welcoming atmosphere are consistent across all our branches, ensuring that you can savor our signature dishes and impeccable hospitality wherever you choose to dine
History
In the vibrant year of 2000, Antonio Rossi embarked on a passionate culinary journey through the heart of Italy, and upon returning to Sydney, he brought the magic of Italian cuisine to life with 'Tavola Italiano.' Antonio's love for the rich traditions and flavors of Italy led to the establishment of our beloved trattoria in the heart of Sydney. For over two decades, 'Tavola Italiano' has been a haven for those seeking an authentic taste of Italy, blending traditional Italian techniques with the freshest local ingredients. Antonio Rossi's vision and dedication continue to make 'Tavola Italiano' a cherished destination for Italian cuisine enthusiasts in Sydney, inviting all to savor the essence of Italy with every dish
